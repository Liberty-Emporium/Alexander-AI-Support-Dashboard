1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

Alexander AI Integrated Solutions ("Provider") agrees to design, develop, and deploy a professional website for Serenity Therapeutic Massage Spa ("Client"). Services include:

  • Custom website design and development (7 pages)
  • Mobile-responsive layout for all devices
  • Online booking integration via MassageBook
  • Google Maps integration and local SEO setup
  • Contact form and business information
  • Deployment to production with custom domain
  • 30 days of post-launch support and bug fixes
2. PAYMENT TERMS

The total cost for the website project is $3,000.00 (Three Thousand Dollars).

Down Payment (due at signing):$1,000.00
Monthly Payment:$200.00
Number of Payments:10 months
Total Financed Amount:$2,000.00
Total Project Cost:$3,000.00

The down payment of $1,000.00 is due upon execution of this agreement. Monthly payments of $200.00 shall begin on the first day of the month following the project start date and continue for ten (10) consecutive months until the financed amount of $2,000.00 is paid in full, for a total project cost of $3,000.00.

3. INTEREST ON FINANCED AMOUNT

Any portion of the financed amount that is not paid in full by the agreed payment schedule shall accrue interest at a rate of four percent (4%) per annum, compounded monthly on the remaining unpaid balance. Interest shall begin accruing on any payment that is more than ten (10) days past due and shall continue until the balance is paid in full.

For example: If a monthly payment of $200.00 is 30 days past due, interest at 4% per annum will be calculated on the outstanding balance for the number of days the payment remains unpaid.

4. LATE PAYMENT POLICY

Payments are due on the first (1st) day of each month. A grace period of ten (10) calendar days is provided. Payments received after the grace period will be considered late and subject to the interest terms outlined in Section 3. Provider reserves the right to suspend website hosting services if payment is more than thirty (30) days past due, until all outstanding balances including accrued interest are resolved.

5. PROJECT TIMELINE

Provider will begin work within five (5) business days of receiving the down payment and signed agreement. Estimated project completion is four (4) to six (6) weeks from the start date, subject to timely provision of content and feedback by Client.

6. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES

Client agrees to:

  • Provide all necessary content (text, images, logos) within 10 days of signing
  • Respond to design mockups and revision requests within 5 business days
  • Set up and maintain domain name and hosting account (if applicable)
  • Maintain MassageBook account for booking integration
  • Review and approve final website before launch
7. REVISIONS

The project includes up to two (2) rounds of revisions to the initial design. Additional revisions beyond the included two rounds will be billed at $75.00 per hour. Content updates (text, images) after launch are not included in the revision rounds.

8. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Upon receipt of full payment ($3,000.00), all rights, title, and interest in the completed website design shall transfer to Client. Provider retains the right to display the work in their portfolio and marketing materials. Any custom code, frameworks, or libraries developed by Provider and used across multiple projects remain the property of Provider and are licensed to Client for use on this project.

9. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this agreement with written notice. If Client terminates after work has begun, the down payment is non-refundable and Client is responsible for payment for all work completed to date. If Provider terminates, a pro-rata refund of the down payment for uncompleted work shall be issued.

10. WARRANTY & SUPPORT

Provider warrants that the website will be free from material defects for thirty (30) days following launch. During this period, Provider will correct any bugs or issues at no additional cost. This warranty does not cover issues arising from Client modifications, third-party service changes, or hosting environment problems.

11. GENERAL PROVISIONS

This agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties. Any modifications must be made in writing and signed by both parties. This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of North Carolina. Any disputes shall be resolved in Randolph County, North Carolina.

12. MONTHLY HOSTING FEE

Upon full payment of the website project cost ($3,000.00), Client agrees to a recurring monthly hosting fee of $20.00 per month for continued website hosting, maintenance, and support services. This hosting fee shall begin on the first day of the month following the completion of the project payment schedule and shall continue on a month-to-month basis until terminated by either party with thirty (30) days written notice.

The hosting fee includes: server hosting and uptime monitoring, SSL certificate management, regular software updates and security patches, up to two (2) minor content updates per month, and email support with 48-hour response time. Major feature additions or redesigns are not included and will be quoted separately.

Late hosting fees are subject to the same interest terms outlined in Section 3 (four percent per annum, compounded monthly, after a ten-day grace period). Provider reserves the right to suspend hosting services if payment is more than thirty (30) days past due.
