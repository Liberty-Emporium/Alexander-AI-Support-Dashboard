🤖 Agent Zero

Agent Zero is a Python app. It runs directly on your computer — no virtual machines, no complicated setup. Pick your operating system and follow 3 steps.

What computer do you have?

1

Open PowerShell

Click the Windows Start button → type PowerShell → click it to open. It looks like a dark window with text.

💡 Tip: Right-click PowerShell and choose "Run as Administrator" for best results.
2

Copy this command and paste it in, then press Enter

irm https://ps.agent-zero.ai | iex
💡 To paste in PowerShell: right-click anywhere in the window, or press Ctrl+V.
3

Wait for it to finish, then open your browser

When the install is done you'll see a success message. Then open your browser and go to:

http://localhost:80
✅ Agent Zero is running! Add your API key in Settings and you're ready to go.
1

Open Terminal

Press Cmd + Space → type Terminal → press Enter. A dark window will open.

2

Copy this command and paste it in, then press Enter

curl -fsSL https://bash.agent-zero.ai | bash
💡 To paste in Terminal: press Cmd+V.
3

Wait for it to finish, then open your browser

When done, open your browser and go to:

http://localhost:80
✅ Agent Zero is running! Add your API key in Settings and you're ready to go.

🪄 Hermes Workspace

Hermes runs completely on your computer — no Docker, no cloud, your data stays private. Pick your operating system below.

What computer do you have?

1

Open PowerShell as Administrator

Click the Windows Start button → type PowerShell → right-click it → click "Run as Administrator" → click Yes.

2

Copy this command and paste it in, then press Enter

This installs everything automatically — Node.js, Git, and Hermes Workspace — and creates a desktop shortcut.

irm https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Liberty-Emporium/Hermes-Workspace-Alexander-AI/main/install-hermes-windows.ps1 | iex
💡 To paste: right-click in the PowerShell window, or press Ctrl+V.
3

When it finishes — double-click "Start Hermes" on your Desktop

The installer puts a shortcut right on your desktop. Double-click it and Hermes opens in your browser automatically.

✅ That's it! Every time you want to use Hermes, just double-click that desktop shortcut.
